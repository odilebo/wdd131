Site Plan: Everyday Ideas That Have Changed Your Life

Site Name

Everyday Ideas- This site explores inventions, concepts, and ideas that have shaped human perception and thought.

odilebo/wdd131/project-home.html

Site Purpose

The website provides an engaging collection of stories, images, and explanations behind everyday ideas that have transformed how we think and live.

Scenarios

  • How have simple inventions like the clock changed our perception of time?
  • What are some overlooked everyday concepts that have a profound impact on society?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2C3E50 (Dark Blue - used for headings)

Accent Color: #E67E22 (Orange - used for buttons and highlights)

Background Color: #ECF0F1 (Light Gray - used for page background)

Typography

Heading Font: Montserrat (used for all headings)

Body Font: Open Sans (used for main content and descriptions)
